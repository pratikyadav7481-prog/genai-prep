Once upon a time, in a green, sunlit meadow, lived a little bunny named Barnaby. Barnaby had very fluffy ears, a tiny twitchy nose, and a huge problem: he never listened to his mother!Every morning, Mama Bunny would say, "Barnaby, my sweet, please stay close to home. The forest beyond our meadow is too big, and the bramble bushes are too prickly."But Barnaby was far too curious to listen. One sunny afternoon, while chasing a beautiful blue butterfly, he hopped right past the safe, familiar meadow and into the edge of the deep, dark woods."Oh, look at that shiny red berry!" Barnaby squeaked, hopping deeper into the trees.Suddenly, Barnaby realized he was completely lost. The trees blocked out the warm sunshine, and he heard strange rustling noises all around him. To make matters worse, he tripped over a pointy, prickly bush and got his soft, fluffy tail completely stuck!"Ouch! Help, please!" Barnaby cried out, his little nose twitching with fear.Luckily, Ollie the Wise Old Owl was watching from a high tree branch. Ollie swooped down gently and helped untangle Barnaby’s tail."The woods are no place for a little bunny who doesn't listen," Ollie hooted kindly but firmly. "Always listen to your mother, little one. The safest adventures are the ones you share together."Barnaby wiggled his tail, wiped away his tears, and promised to listen from now on. He followed Ollie's guiding light all the way back to the cozy meadow, where Mama Bunny was waiting with a warm hug. From that day on, Barnaby always stayed close to home and lived happily ever after.
